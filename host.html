What We Do

We’re building a free, privacy-respecting CCTV network across Sutton and beyond — designed for public safety, not profit.

Fast footage access for police, victims, and DSARs
Privacy masking for private areas
Cameras stored locally, not streamed
Full compliance with UK GDPR laws
Free to host, fully managed by our team

Why Host a Camera?

By hosting a camera, you help protect your neighborhood while maintaining complete privacy. Cameras only monitor public or semi-public spaces — never your home or garden. All footage is stored locally and managed ethically.

No cost to you — we provide everything
No tech skills required — fully installed & managed
View live footage from your area (if applicable)
Helps deter crime and gather evidence quickly

How It Works

Once you apply to host a camera, we’ll assess your location for suitability. If approved, our team will handle the entire installation and configuration process.

We install and configure the camera
We apply privacy zones and GDPR compliance
Footage is retained for 31 days maximum
Only authorized users can request or view footage
