Footage Request

We comply fully with the UK GDPR, and individuals have the right to request any personal data captured by our cameras. You may submit a Subject Access Request (SAR) for footage in which you believe you are identifiable.

To submit a SAR:

You must provide valid photographic government-issued ID.

Once your identity is verified, relevant footage will be reviewed and shared if available.

If no matching individual is found in the footage, your request may be rejected.

Footage may be redacted to protect the privacy of others.

We retain footage for a maximum of 31 days, and we aim to respond to requests as quickly as possible within that time.

You may also submit an Incident-Based Request if you believe a crime or serious incident occurred.

This must be submitted within 15 days of the incident.

This allows sufficient time for us to retrieve and review footage from multiple nearby cameras if necessary.

Requests made after 15 days may not be fulfilled if the footage is no longer retrievable in time.
