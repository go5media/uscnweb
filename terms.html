Section 2: Terms for the General Public

If you walk through or appear in an area covered by one of our cameras, the following terms apply:

1. Purpose of Monitoring
Footage is recorded for the purposes of crime prevention and public safety only.

We do not use or share footage for commercial purposes.

2. Subject Access Requests (SARs)
If you believe you have been recorded and wish to access footage of yourself, you may submit a Data Subject Access Request (DSAR).

To process your request, you must provide:

A clear description of the time, date, and location

Valid photographic ID to verify your identity

Once verified, we will:

Check whether you are present in the footage

If not present, inform you accordingly

If present and no other people are visible, we will provide the unedited footage

If other people are present, we will apply blurring/redaction before providing a copy

Your ID will be securely deleted after verification and will not be retained.

3. Footage Removal Requests
We do not delete footage simply upon request unless required by law.

This is to preserve the integrity of our public safety purpose and ensure potential evidence is retained.

4. Data Retention
Footage is stored for a maximum of 31 days or until local storage becomes full, whichever comes sooner.

All footage is stored securely and is automatically deleted once it exceeds the retention period.
